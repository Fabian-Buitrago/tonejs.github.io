MultiPlayer → Tone

Tone.MultiPlayer implements a "fire and forget" style buffer player. This is very good for short samples like drum hits, sound effects and instruments samples. Unlike Tone.Player, Tone.MultiPlayer cannot loop samples or change any attributes of a playing sample.

new Tone.MultiPlayer (buffers)
buffers {Object}

An object with sample names as the keys and either urls or Tone.Buffers as the values.
